Why Top of India Offers the Best Indian Cuisine in Spokane Valley

Top of India stands out not only for its flavorful dishes but also for its dedication to preserving the authenticity of India’s rich culinary traditions. Here’s what makes it the best Indian cuisinein Spokane Valley:

1. Authentic, Traditional Recipes

Indian cuisine is known for its diversity, and Top of India takes great pride in offering authentic dishes from various regions of India. The chefs at Top of India are not just skilled; they bring with them years of expertise in preparing traditional Indian food. Hailing from different parts of the country, each chef adds their unique touch to the menu, ensuring that every dish is a true representation of its regional roots.

From the creamy, flavorful curries of the North to the tangy, spice-filled dishes of the South, the menu at Top of India reflects the rich culinary tapestry of India. Whether you’re enjoying the mild flavors of a Mughlai Korma or the fiery spices of a Goan Vindaloo, each bite tells a story of India’s diverse food culture. The restaurant’s commitment to using traditional recipes, passed down through generations, guarantees that you’re experiencing the best Indian cuisine available.

2. A Diverse and Expansive Menu

One of the standout features of Indian cuisine is its incredible variety. At Top of India, their extensive menu offers something for everyone, from vegetarians to meat lovers, from those who enjoy mild dishes to those who love a good challenge with spice. The menu is thoughtfully crafted to highlight the full range of Indian cooking, including:

  • Curries and Gravies: Indian curries are known for their depth of flavor, and Top of India offers a variety that caters to all tastes. Whether you prefer a rich, creamy Butter Chicken, a spicy Chicken Vindaloo, or a vegetarian-friendly Paneer Tikka Masala, there’s a curry for every palate. These curries are often paired with naan (soft, fluffy flatbread) or rice, both of which complement the bold flavors perfectly.
  • Tandoori Specialties: The tandoor oven, a cylindrical clay oven used in traditional Indian cooking, imparts a smoky, charred flavor to meats and breads. At Top of India, dishes cooked in the tandoor are a must-try. Their Chicken Tikka, Seekh Kebabs, and Tandoori Paneer are marinated with spices and grilled to perfection, offering a crispy exterior and a juicy interior. If you’re in the mood for a hearty, flavorful dish, their Tandoori Mixed Grill is a great choice, featuring an assortment of meats.
  • Biryani and Rice Dishes: Biryani is one of India’s most iconic dishes, and Top of India’s version doesn’t disappoint. Prepared with fragrant basmati rice, tender pieces of chicken, lamb, or vegetables, and a variety of aromatic spices, their biryanis are rich in flavor and a perfect meal on their own. The restaurant also serves other rice dishes, such as Pulao and Jeera Rice, which are ideal for pairing with curries.
  • Vegetarian Delights: Vegetarian Indian food is rich in flavor and texture, offering a wide range of options for plant-based eaters. Top of India’s Chana Masala (chickpeas in a spicy tomato-based sauce), Aloo Gobi (a potato and cauliflower curry), and Baingan Bharta (mashed eggplant cooked with spices) are just a few examples of their mouthwatering vegetarian offerings. Whether you’re a vegetarian or not, you’ll find these dishes to be incredibly flavorful and satisfying.
  • Indian Street Food and Appetizers: Indian street food is an essential part of the cuisine, offering bold, tangy, and spicy flavors in every bite. Top of India’s appetizers like Samosas, Pakoras, and Pani Puri offer a great start to your meal. The Samosas (crispy pastry filled with spiced potatoes and peas) and Onion Bhajis (fried fritters) are particularly popular, as they perfectly capture the essence of Indian street food.
  • Sweets and Desserts: No Indian meal is complete without a touch of sweetness, and Top of India’s selection of desserts does not disappoint. Gulab Jamun, soft dough balls soaked in rose-scented syrup, and Kheer, a sweet, creamy rice pudding flavored with cardamom and saffron, are two of the most beloved Indian desserts. They are a perfect way to end your meal on a high note.
